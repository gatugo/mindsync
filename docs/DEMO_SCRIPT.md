# MindSync - Product Demo Script

**Target Duration:** 60-90 Seconds
**Tone:** Calm, Focused, Professional yet accessible.

---

## 1. The Hook (0:00 - 0:15)
**Visual:**
- Start with the "Stitch" view (empty or chaotic timeline).
- Cut to the "MindSync" logo/landing page.

**Audio (Voiceover):**
"Do you ever feel like your day is a battle between what you *have* to do and what you *want* to do? ... Meet **MindSync**."

## 2. The Core Concept (0:15 - 0:30)
**Visual:**
- Show the **Timeline** view.
- Hover over the "Adult" (Work), "Child" (Play), and "Rest" categories.
- Show the "Balance Score" adjusting as you add tasks.

**Audio:**
"MindSync isn't just a to-do list. It's a balance engine. It helps you harmonize your 'Adult' responsibilities with your 'Inner Child's' need for play and your body's need for 'Rest'."

## 3. The Workflow (0:30 - 0:50)
**Visual:**
- **Kanban Board**: Drag a task from "To Do" to "In Progress".
- **AI Coach**: Open the chat. Type "I'm feeling overwhelmed."
- Show the AI responding with a broken-down plan or a comforting message.

**Voiceover:**
"Visualize your flow with the Kanban board. And when you get stuck? The built-in **AI Coach** is there to help you break down tasks or just get you back on track."

## 4. Feature Highlight: Natural Language (0:50 - 1:05)
**Visual:**
- Open the "New Task" modal.
- Type: *"Gym tomorrow at 5pm"* -> Show it auto-parsing the time.
- Click "Add".

**Voiceover:**
"Capture ideas as fast as you think. mindless natural language entry means you spend less time planning, and more time doing."

## 5. Outro (1:05 - 1:20)
**Visual:**
- Show the "Dark Mode" toggle (Day/Night transition).
- Final shot: MindSync Dashboard with a high "Balance Score".
- Text on Screen: **MindSync** | *Sync. Focus. Flow.*

**Voiceover:**
"Stop Managing. Start Syncing. Try MindSync today."
